Bike Store Revenue Dashboard

Dataset

Dataset was retrieved from a database provided by the client. Data was altered to protect data privacy and it is available here.

Objective

The objective of this project was to create an interactive dashboard for a client's bike store to visualize and analyze revenue trends across multiple dimensions such as years, states, stores, and bike categories.

Task

  • Retrieve data from the database using SQL.
  • Prepare the data for visualization by aggregating and structuring it appropriately.
  • Design and develop an interactive dashboard using Tableau.
  • Incorporate key metrics such as revenues by year, profitability by state, revenue trends, revenue per store, revenue per bike category, and identification of top customers.

    • Action

    Data Retrieval and Preparation: Utilized SQL queries to extract relevant data from the database, including revenue, store information, bike categories, and customer data. Cleaned and transformed the data as necessary for analysis and visualization. Here are the SQL queries.

    Dashboard Creation: Developed the dashboard using Tableau, incorporating various visualizations to represent the revenue insights effectively. Created summary views for revenues and sales by year, profitability analysis by state, revenue trends over time, revenue per store, revenue per bike category, and identification of top customers.

    Results

  • The interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the client's bike store revenues across different dimensions.
  • Users can easily visualize revenue trends over the years, identify top-performing states and stores, analyze profitability, and understand revenue distribution across bike categories.
  • The dashboard enables quick identification of key insights and facilitates data-driven decision-making for the client's business.
