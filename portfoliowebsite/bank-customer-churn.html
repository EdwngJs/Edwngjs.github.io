Bank Customer Churn Overview

Dataset

Data used for this dashboard belongs to a fictional dataset about bank's customers. Dataset is available on github.

About the dashboard

Interactive dashboard built with Tableau. Here I explored bank's customer data to find potential factors leading to churn in their customers by visually comparing churn rate with different categories. This dashboard depicts an overview of this graphical comparison.

Here are few things covered in this dashboard:
  1. Total Clients.
  2. Total churned customers.
  3. Churn rate: by tenure, by country, by age and by number of products owned.
