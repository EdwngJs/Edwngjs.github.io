Data Crime Dashboard

Dataset

Data used for this dashboard belongs to a fictional employee data from the HR department of a company. Dataset is available on github.

About the dashboard

Interactive HR dashboard with raw data from two CSV files (HR data and employee data). Here are few things covered in this dashboard:

  1. Basic HR data such as total employees, gender proportion, average age.
  2. KPIs: Years of service, performance rate, job satisfaction, average salary versus market.
  3. Promotion status details for each employee.
