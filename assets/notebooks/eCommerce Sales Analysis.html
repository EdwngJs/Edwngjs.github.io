At first glance, we observed the following anomalies:

  • Unusual quantities (-80995.0 , 80995.0) and unit prices (-11062.06, 0, 38970.0) values.
  • Inconsistency in Stockcodes (DOT, POST)
  • Missing CustomerIDs.
  • Different / missing descriptions for same StockCode.

However, most of these seem to be caused by entry errors and new records were created to adjust them. Negative quantity values could be a 'return transaction' in which the item was returned. We could go deeper to identify more anomalies but let's keep it simple. Therefore, those records with null values could be deleted for the analysis.
